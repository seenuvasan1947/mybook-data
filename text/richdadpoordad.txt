"Rich Dad Poor Dad" by Robert Kiyosaki is a personal finance book that teaches about financial literacy, money management, and investing. The book has 10 chapters, and below is a summary of each chapter:

Chapter 1: Rich Dad, Poor Dad

The author introduces his two dads – his real dad, who was an educated man but struggled financially, and his best friend's dad, who was a wealthy entrepreneur. The author contrasts the money mindsets of the two fathers and explains how the advice he received from his rich dad changed his life. He also introduces the concept of financial literacy, which he believes is the key to building wealth.

Chapter 2: The Rich Don't Work for Money

The author explains how working for money can become a trap and why it's important to have money work for you instead. He introduces the concept of assets and liabilities, and how understanding the difference between the two is crucial to financial success. He also emphasizes the importance of developing a strong mindset and taking responsibility for one's own financial future.

Chapter 3: Why Teach Financial Literacy?

In this chapter, the author explains how the education system often fails to teach financial literacy and why it's important to seek out financial education on your own. He also stresses the importance of parents teaching their children about money and investing in their financial education.

Chapter 4: The History of Taxes and the Power of Corporations

The author discusses how the tax system works and how it benefits the wealthy and corporations. He explains how understanding taxes is crucial to financial success and how starting a business or investing in real estate can be advantageous in terms of taxes.

Chapter 5: The Rich Invent Money

The author emphasizes the importance of creativity and problem-solving in creating wealth. He explains how the most successful entrepreneurs have found innovative ways to create value and wealth. He also explains how financial education and a strong mindset are essential to identifying opportunities and creating wealth.

Chapter 6: Work to Learn – Don't Work for Money

The author stresses the importance of education and experience over earning a high salary. He explains how working to learn, rather than just to earn, can be a powerful way to build wealth over time. He also discusses how the fear of failure can hold people back and why taking risks is necessary for financial success.

Chapter 7: Overcoming Obstacles

The author explains how fear and self-doubt can prevent people from taking risks and pursuing their dreams. He provides tips on how to overcome these obstacles and develop the confidence needed to succeed in business and investing.

Chapter 8: Getting Started

In this chapter, the author explains how to take the first steps towards building wealth, including reducing expenses, creating a financial plan, and developing a strong mindset. He also emphasizes the importance of starting small and gradually building wealth over time.

Chapter 9: Still Want More? Here Are Some To Do's

The author provides practical advice on how to start building wealth, including investing in real estate, starting a business, and investing in stocks and mutual funds. He also discusses the importance of taking action and making a plan to achieve financial goals.

Chapter 10: Ten Steps to Start You on the Road to Riches

In the final chapter, the author provides a summary of the key points covered in the book and outlines ten steps to help readers achieve financial success. These include changing your mindset, finding a mentor, and investing in assets.

Overall, "Rich Dad Poor Dad" provides practical advice on how to build wealth through financial literacy, entrepreneurship, and investing. The book emphasizes the importance of developing a strong mindset, taking risks, and taking responsibility for one's own financial future
